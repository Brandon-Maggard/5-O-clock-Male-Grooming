Our Shop

After working year in the industry and working for other shop owners, Rhonda McNair decided to branch off and take a chance on her dream instead of playing a part in someone else's. In 2017, Rhonda decided to open her own barber shop, and is where 5 O'clock Male Grooming was created. She started small in a shop next to the Sikes Center Mall and had two other barbers to help her set the fondation. Her vision was to create an old-school feel and bring the art of barbering back in the forefront; however, with some modern services included. She created a relaxing environment where you can get a classic but modern cut, while also getting pampered and having a cold one while it happens.
